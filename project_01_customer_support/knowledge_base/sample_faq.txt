# Company Knowledge Base - Sample FAQ

## Product Information

### What is TechFlow CRM?
TechFlow CRM is a cloud-based customer relationship management system designed for small to medium businesses. It helps manage customer interactions, sales pipelines, and marketing campaigns all in one platform.

### What are the main features?
- Contact Management: Store unlimited customer contacts with detailed profiles
- Sales Pipeline: Visual drag-and-drop sales tracking
- Email Integration: Sync with Gmail, Outlook automatically
- Automation: Set up automated follow-up emails and reminders
- Analytics: Real-time dashboards and reports
- Mobile App: iOS and Android apps available

### What integrations are available?
TechFlow CRM integrates with:
- Email: Gmail, Outlook, Office 365
- Calendar: Google Calendar, Outlook Calendar
- Communication: Slack, Microsoft Teams, WhatsApp Business
- Payments: Stripe, PayPal
- Marketing: Mailchimp, HubSpot
- E-commerce: Shopify, WooCommerce

## Account Management

### How do I reset my password?
1. Go to login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for reset link
5. Click link and create new password

### How do I add team members?
1. Go to Settings > Team
2. Click "Add User"
3. Enter their email address
4. Assign role (Admin, Manager, or User)
5. They'll receive an invitation email

### Can I export my data?
Yes! You can export all your data anytime:
1. Go to Settings > Data Export
2. Select what to export (Contacts, Deals, etc.)
3. Choose format (CSV, Excel, JSON)
4. Click "Export"
5. Download file when ready

## Security & Privacy

### Is my data secure?
Yes! We take security seriously:

Data is stored in secure AWS data centers in the United States. Enterprise customers can choose EU or Asia-Pacific regions.

## Troubleshooting

### I can't log in. What should I do?
1. Check if Caps Lock is on
2. Try resetting your password
3. Clear browser cache and cookies
4. Try a different browser
5. Contact support if issue persists

### Emails aren't syncing. How do I fix this?
1. Go to Settings > Email Integration
2. Click "Reconnect" next to your email
3. Grant permissions again
4. Wait 5-10 minutes for sync
5. Contact support if still not working

### The app is running slow. How can I speed it up?
1. Clear browser cache
2. Close unused tabs
3. Check internet connection
4. Update to latest browser version
5. Try incognito/private mode
6. Contact support if problem continues
